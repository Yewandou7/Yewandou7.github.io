最基本的假设是认为可以按照线性很好的拟合, 线性拟合需要的样本量小, 方差小, 在稀疏数据集上也有比较好的应用.

  • 单变量线性回归;

    • 让上式取0, 有
      $$
      \begin{align*}
      \beta_0 &= \overline {y} - \beta \overline{x}\

      L(\beta) &= \sum_{i=1}^N (y_i-\overline{y} - \beta (x_i - \overline {x}))^2\

      \frac{\partial L(\beta)}{\partial \beta} &= -\sum_{i=1}^N (x_i-\overline{x})(y_i-\overline{y} - \beta (x_i-\overline{x}))
      \end{align*}

      \hat{\beta} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^N (x_i-\overline{x})(y_i-\overline{y})}{\sum_{i=1}^N (x_i-\overline{x})^2}
      $$

5. Subset Selection

1. Introduction

​ Two limitations of least squares

  • prediction accuracy

    • low bias and high variance

      -> sacrifice a little bias to reduce the variance

  • interpretation

    • hard to interpret a large number of input features

      ->find a subset of features exhibiting strong effects

想法就是我们要克服两个限制, 使用model selection

  • variable subset selection, shrinkage, dimension reduction.
  • not restricted to linear models

2. Best-subset selection

​ 对, 找到一个大小为的子集, 方案数, 使得最小.

  • 训练所有的方案共, 最后看每个的RSS, 对数据量小的时候才能使用
