Fisher’s Formulation of Discriminant Analysis

    • Where and , we can do the eigen-decomposition s.t.

  • 化简有
    $$
    \begin{align*}
    \log Pr(G=k|X=x) &= -\frac12 (x-\hat\mu_k)^T\hat\Sigma^{-1}(x-\hat\mu_k) + \log\hat\pi_k + Constant\
    &= -\frac12(x-\hat{\mu}_k)^TUD^{-\frac12}(UD^{-\frac12})^T(x-\hat\mu_k)+\log\hat\pi_k+C\
    &= -\frac12(D^{-\frac12}U^Tx-D^{-\frac12}U^T\hat\mu_k)^T(D^{-\frac12}U^Tx-D^{-\frac12}U^T\hat\mu_k)+\log\hat\pi_k+C \
    &= -\frac12(x^-\hat\mu_k^)^T(x^-\hat\mu_k^)+\log\hat\pi_k+C\
    &= -\frac12||x^-\hat\mu_k^||^2+\log\hat\pi_k+C
    \end{align*}
    $$

  • 如此最大化也是分类的结果, 即
    $$
    \hat{G}(x) = \text{argmax}{k\in\mathcal{G}}\delta_k(x) = \text{argmin}{k\in\mathcal{G}}\frac12||x^-\hat{\mu}_k^||^2-\log\hat\pi_k
    $$

接下来这块好像是叫主成分分析(PCA) 不过也好像不叫, anyway就写一下

  • Goal: Find such that the between class variance is maximized relative to the within class variance. 即
    $$
    \max_a \frac{(\mu_1^-\mu_2^)^2}{\sigma_1^{*2}+\sigma_2^{*2}} = \max_a\frac{a^T\biggl((\mu_1-\mu_2)(\mu_1-\mu_2)^T\biggr)a}{a^T\left(\sum_{k=1}^2\sum_{g_i=k}(x_i-\mu_k)(x_i-\mu_k)^T\right)a}
    $$

  • 记, , 那么我们实际上就是要最大化形如这样的瑞利商(Rayleigh quotient)
